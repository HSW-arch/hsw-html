Company Introduction

Zhongshan Hongshengwei Technology Development Co., Ltd. is a leading manufacturer in the air conditioning grille industry, as recognized on Alibaba's international platform. Situated in Zhongshan City, Guangdong Province, our factory has garnered fifteen years of production expertise and stands as a powerful, custom-oriented enterprise specializing in the production and development of grid outlets, including grilles, diffusers, vents, and flexible air ducts.

We are committed to offering comprehensive solutions by integrating development, design, production, sales, and one-stop services. We have embraced advanced equipment such as automated production and packaging lines, ensuring high efficiency, quality, and cost-effectiveness. We undertake engineering projects, constantly striving to improve in order to provide superior products and services.
